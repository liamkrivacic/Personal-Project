Art is one of my hobbies. I love exploring different mediums and styles, and honestly just
wanted to show it off. Some of this work was recognised through an **ARTEXPRESS nomination**
and selected for exhibition at **Tamworth Regional Gallery**, which I'm proud of, but mostly
I just enjoy the process.
